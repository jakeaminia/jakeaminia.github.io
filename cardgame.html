Card Game

Rules of the Game

The cards are valued from 1-13, with A=1, J=11, Q=12, K=13 and no jokers. 2 cards are placed face up in the middle, and their values are multiplied together. Each player picks 4 cards. The goal is to get a number closest to a multiple of the middle product. A player 'gets' a number by representing the digits of that number with their cards. 2-digit cards carry their first digit to the left, as when performing addition on paper. If two or more players get the same distance from a multiple, the higher number wins. No calculators allowed!

Examples: (A 9 5) is 195, (A 10 5) is 205, (A Q 5 2) is 2,252, (K 3 J 7) is 13,417
